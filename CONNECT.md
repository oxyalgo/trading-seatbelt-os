# 🔗 Connect

The Trading Seatbelt is **free and fully local**. It runs on your machine, needs no account, and **sends nothing anywhere**. You never have to give an email to use every feature.

If you *want* more, these are optional and opt-in:

### 📦 Prop-firm rule packs, kept current
Firms change their rules constantly (drawdowns, contract limits, payout terms). Get the maintained packs for **TakeProfitTrader, Topstep, Apex** (and more as the community adds them), plus a heads-up when a firm changes a rule that could fail your account.

→ **Free:** https://oxyalgo.com/seatbelt *(drop your email so we can send you the updates — nothing else)*

### 💬 Join the community
Funded traders sharing rule packs, catching each other's tilt, and keeping the seatbelt sharp.

→ **Community:** https://oxyalgo.com

---

**What we collect, plainly:** only the email you type in *if* you request the rule packs, used only to send rule-pack updates and occasional OXY news. Unsubscribe anytime. We never sell your data, and we never read anything you don't hand us on purpose.
