# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone — especially beginners who are just learning how to protect themselves.

## Our standards

**Do:**

- Be kind, patient, and helpful.
- Assume good intent and give constructive feedback.
- Respect that people are here to learn.

**Don't:**

- Harass, insult, or demean anyone.
- Post scams, "signal group" pitches, guaranteed-profit claims, or account-management offers.
- Share anyone's private information.
- Give personalized financial advice — this project is educational only.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and PRs that break this code, and may ban repeat or serious offenders.

To report a problem, contact the maintainers privately (see the repo profile/README for a current contact). Reports are handled confidentially.

This Code of Conduct is adapted in spirit from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
